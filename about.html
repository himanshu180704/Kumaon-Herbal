Who We Are

From the Wild Hills of Uttarakhand

Kumaon Herbal was founded with a single belief — that the mountains of Uttarakhand hold the most potent, purest natural ingredients on earth. Nestled at altitudes between 1,500 and 3,000 metres, the Kumaon region of the Himalayas is home to wild herbs, rare berries, ancient spice routes, and generations of traditional wisdom.

We started as a small family venture in the hills of Almora, collecting herbs the way our grandmothers did — by hand, in season, with care. Today, we work with over 200 local farmers and forest foragers to bring you products that are 100% natural, chemical-free, and deeply rooted in Himalayan heritage.

Every product we make carries the soul of Kumaon — its clean air, its sacred forests, its slow rhythms. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just pure goodness, bottled and jarred with love.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

🌱

Farm to Bottle

We work directly with small-scale farmers in the Kumaon hills. No middlemen means better prices for farmers and fresher products for you.

🚫

Zero Additives

We never use artificial colours, flavours, preservatives, or chemical enhancers. Our ingredient lists are short and every word on them is honest.

🏔️

Himalayan Purity

Our herbs grow in pristine mountain air, nourished by glacial streams. The altitude and ecosystem create a potency you simply can't replicate elsewhere.

🤝

Community First

We reinvest in local communities — funding schools, supporting women-led herb-cultivation groups, and preserving traditional knowledge.

♻️

Eco Packaging

Every bottle, jar, and mailer is recyclable or biodegradable. We're committed to leaving the mountains as clean as we found them.

🔬

Quality Tested

All products go through rigorous quality checks before dispatch. What reaches you meets the highest standards of purity and safety.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

2014

The Beginning

Founded in a small kitchen in Almora, Uttarakhand, making 5 products for local markets using family recipes.

2016

Farmer Network

Partnered with 50+ Kumaoni farmers to source herbs and ingredients directly, ensuring quality at the root.

2018

FSSAI Certification

Received FSSAI food safety certification. Expanded product line to 25+ SKUs across juices, pickles & herbal items.

2020

Online Launch

Launched our e-commerce store during the pandemic. Reached customers across 18 Indian states within 6 months.

2023

25,000 Customers

Crossed 25,000 happy customers. Won the Uttarakhand Udyog Ratna award for promoting local agri-products.

2025

50+ Products, Pan India

Expanded to 50+ products with delivery across all major Indian cities. Began exporting to the UK & UAE.
